Internship 2024 at Rutherford Appleton Laboratory, United Kingdom Research and Innovation, Hawell, UK
======

In the summer of 2024, Dr. Nelson Diaz with Pontificia Universidad Católica de Valparaíso (PUCV) did a week visit at Tampere University in the computational imaging group of Professor Karen Egiazarian and a one-month internship at Rutherford Appleton Laboratory, United Kingdom Research and Innovation at Harwell, UK. During the internship, Nelson Diaz worked on a new wavefront coding for multispectral imaging. He worked with his doctoral colleague, Dr. Samuel Pinilla.
